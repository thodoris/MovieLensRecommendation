MOVIE APP

This application allow you to discover new movies based on your rating on movies you like (or dislike)!

Start by typing the title of your favorites movies to add them to MY MOVIES section.

Then rate at least (3) movies and press the GO button.

That's it. Your recommendation will appear to the MY RECOMMENDATIONS section.

 

Happy rating!
